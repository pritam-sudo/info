Package Details

Embark on a 7-day Family that blends luxury, relaxation, and adventure. Start your journey in Port Blair, where you’ll explore the historic Cellular Jail and enjoy a captivating Sound & Light Show. Dive into the azure waters of North Bay Island, and discover the enchanting ruins of Ross Island
Sail to Havelock Island, where you’ll unwind on the stunning Radhanagar Beach, rated one of Asia’s best. Indulge in water sports and snorkeling at Elephant Beach, creating unforgettable memories together.
Next, escape to Neil Island for serene moments on secluded beaches like Laxmanpur and Bharatpur. End your journey with a leisurely return to Port Blair, where you can explore local markets before your departure. This premium package ensures an intimate and luxurious experience, perfect for Familyers.

Details

01

DAY 1 : Arrival at Port Blair

Morning/Afternoon: Arrive at Port Blair airport and transfer to your hotel.
Afternoon: Visit the Cellular Jail, a key historical site from the British colonial period.
Evening: Enjoy the Sound & Light Show at Cellular Jail, narrating the story of India's freedom struggle.

  • Private Transport
  • Water,Dinner andSnacks

02

DAY 2 :Port Blair to Baratang Island

Early Morning: Depart for Baratang Island, starting with a scenic drive through the dense tropical forest and Jarawa Tribal Reserve.
Morning: Explore the Limestone Caves, marveling at the natural rock formations.
Afternoon: Visit the unique Mud Volcanoes on Baratang Island.
Evening: Return to Port Blair for an overnight stay.

  • Private Transport
  • Water,Dinner andSnacks

03

DAY 3 :Port Blair to Havelock Island

Morning: Take an early ferry to Havelock Island (Swaraj Dweep).
Afternoon: Relax at Radhanagar Beach, famous for its pristine white sand and clear blue waters.
Evening: Enjoy the stunning sunset at Radhanagar Beach, one of Asia's best beaches.

  • Private Transport
  • Water,Dinner andSnacks

04

DAY 4 :Havelock Island

Morning: Head to Elephant Beach for snorkeling and other water sports. Experience the vibrant coral reefs and colorful marine life.
Afternoon: Leisurely explore more of Havelock Island or simply unwind at your resort.

  • Private Transport
  • Water,Dinner andSnacks

05

DAY 5 : Next Day Havelock Island

Morning: Take a guided trek to the beautiful Kalapathar Beach, known for its long stretch of black rocks and calm waters.
Afternoon: Enjoy leisure time or explore other parts of the island at your own pace.
Evening: Relax at your accommodation, soaking in the serene ambiance of Havelock.

  • Private Transport
  • Water,Dinner andSnacks

06

DAY 6 :Havelock Island to Neil Island

Morning: Catch a ferry to Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep), a more tranquil and less commercialized spot.
Afternoon: Explore Laxmanpur Beach, Bharatpur Beach, and the Natural Bridge. Each site offers a unique landscape and quietude.
Evening: Overnight stay on Neil Island.

  • Private Transport
  • Water,Dinner andSnacks

07

DAY 7 : Neil Island to Port Blair & Departure

Morning: Enjoy a relaxed morning at Neil Island, with some time for a beach walk or swim.
Late Morning: Take a ferry back to Port Blair.
Afternoon: Spend any remaining time exploring Port Blair’s markets or visiting any last sights.
Evening: Transfer to the airport for your departure.

  • Private Transport
  • Water,Dinner andSnacks
